What we do

We are a Third-Party Administration (TPA) and a member of Naco Insurance Group. We manage medical schemes on behalf of insurance companies, self-dunded schemes, corporation, governmental institutions and other entities. We do this locally, regionally and globally.

Because our clients are our first priority, and because we genuiely have their best intereets at heart, we know how to listen, find solutions proactively and be responsive to their needs. We consider this our duty and part of the long-term commitment.
